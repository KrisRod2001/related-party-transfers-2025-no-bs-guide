Who Gets Zero Stamp Duty Right Now (December 2025)

100% EXEMPT (all states):

  • Married spouses ↔ spouses
  • De facto partners (registered or 2+ years living together)
  • Domestic partners (VIC/NSW/ACT registered relationships)

PARTIAL OR FULL CONCESSION:

  • Parent → child (principal residence only – SA, TAS, NT)
  • Family trust → beneficiary (90%+ same people)
  • Company → shareholder (grouping rules)

FULL MARKET DUTY (no concession):

  • Friends, cousins, uncles/aunts
  • Investment property to child (most states)
  • Any transfer where money actually changes hands

The One Mistake That Costs $50k+ (Happens Weekly)

Using a normal Contract for Sale instead of the exemption transfer form.

Revenue offices see “contract” → automatically assess full market duty → you pay $40k–$150k unnecessarily.

Fix: Use the state-specific Transfer form + tick the correct exemption box.

Exact 7-Step Process (Takes 3–8 Weeks)

  1. Decide who the property is going to
  2. Get proof of relationship ready (marriage cert, statutory declaration, etc.)
  3. Fill out the Transfer form (not a contract)
  4. Lodge online with revenue office → instant or 1–3 day approval
  5. Sign transfer (no cooling-off)
  6. Settle electronically via PEXA
  7. Title updates in 24–48 hours
